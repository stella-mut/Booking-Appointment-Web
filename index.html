Questions, answered

Good to
know.

Still wondering something? Bring your questions to your appointment — we're always happy to talk things through.

How long does a check-up take? +

A standard appointment usually takes around 30–45 minutes, depending on your needs. We always leave time for questions.

Do you accept children? +

Absolutely. We welcome children and focus on making their visits gentle, positive and pressure-free.

What should I bring to my first appointment? +

Just yourself and any relevant medical or dental history you have. If you have recent X-rays, bring those too.

Do you offer emergency appointments? +

Yes. Call our emergency line and we'll guide you on the next best step and availability.

How often should I visit the dentist? +

For most people, every six months is a good starting point, but we'll recommend a schedule based on your individual needs.

Can I book online? +

Yes. Use the booking flow above to request a time. We'll follow up to confirm the appointment.
